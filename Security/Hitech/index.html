The company officers integrated and comprehensive management Consultancy to cater the needs of the Corporate, Factories, Banking, IT, Communication, Hospitality, Protection to the Govt. Training

We are of the opinion that the Training is essential for optimum utilization of an individual and to his personnel growth and to meet the client’s requirements. It brings in effectiveness and to make himself suitable to tackle the various situations that he may come across at the site. Prior to deployment we train our security personnel as per requirement of duly location. We normally need 30 days to deploy our security staff depending upon the size of security requirements so that we train and mould them to meet the requirements of the client. We also arrange for the refresher course every six month. We have special training modules for each and every cadre of our security staff. All the personnel of HIECH are trained at our in-house training center at Chennai. Which follows strict defense and security training standards and this training equips each security guard to handle any situation. Further Training is imparted at client’s place on monthly basis.
