About Us

Sri Bhavan's Foods Pvt Ltd, is the hospitality vertical of the hitech group, incorporated as a separate company for better focus on Catering customer service. Our traditional business model is based on the accomplishment of customer delight, by consistently serving tasty food with custom / individual menu, clean preparations in hygienic environment, and competitive prices. Our performance is built on time-tested principles: offer quality at a reasonable price. Founded by an army veteran decorated medalist in operation Vijay (Kargil war), Bhavan’s business philosophy imbibes the inherited discipline, integrity, loyalty, more values, ethics and the contemporary value system inculcated by the armed services.

  1. Specializing in outdoor catering to industries with current integrated focused operations in oragadam, Sriperumbudur, Irungattukottai, Pillaipakkam Industrial Belt.
  2. Proven ability to cater for 6000 people providing services ranging from breakfast, hot lunch, and dinner service and that extra touch when it comes to those special corporate requirements.
  3. Group has decade long and proud record of providing proven personable and proactive quality services to major enterprises.
  4. Key management personnel provide a wealth of experience and through their ‘hands on’ approach with continued support to site operations and clients.
  5. Bhavan provide it service at Sriperumbudur, oragadam, Sunguvarchathiram, Irungattukottai, and Thiruvallur
  6. Around 30km rations providing the services

Profile

A.Rajaram - Managing Director, is the founder and Chairman of SRI BHAVAN'S FOODS PVT LTD, Director R.Bhavani, Joint Director R.Karthik Raja taking care of Administration and statutory.

AR had acquired skills in all aspects of management, strategy, customer orientating during his illustrious stint with the Indian army, serving as commando, special weapons instructor, Bomb Disposals, and Border Intelligence. His empathetic assessment of the customer needs, systematic approach to execution and respect for people is the key of the success of the organization which has high acquisition rate in terms of outdoor catering, clients in sriperumbudur and oragadam industrial Belt.

He is responsible for the overall working of the company and is instrumental in making strategic decisions for the company.

The management team consists of professionals in hospitality industry, Finance, Administration, Logistics, Utilities and with capability, experience and track record. They provide round the clock support to all the stake holders for a smooth and hassle free running of the entire operations.
